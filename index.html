Welcome, to Paxjah.art!

My name is Angel (aka Paxjah) - my pronouns are they/them/theirs

I'm a surrealist artist, and cat-mum to three floofy meow-voids, living in the beautiful British countryside.

I create traditional art using acrylic paint on any canvas, and digital pixel art.

I wear many badges - Autistic, ADHD, bipolar, disabled, chronically-ill - to provode context for my art.
